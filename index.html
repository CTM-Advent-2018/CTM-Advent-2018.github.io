CTM Advent 2018

A collection of (mostly) hardcore mode mini complete the monument style maps made for the 2018 winter holiday season. Each map consists of two required objectives (red and green wool), as well as a bonus objective (music disc) for those with an inclination for challenge and exploration. Maps should be comfortably beatable in a single sitting and are also designed with speedrunning in mind, although it not intended as the primary play style.

Additionally, most maps will focus around a particular unique item, mechanic, or environmental challenge.
